About This Course

T-FLAWS is a systematic flock health and welfare assessment framework for commercial poultry operations across Canada. By evaluating Toes, Feathers, Legs, Activity, Weight, and Skin together, producers can identify problems early and respond with evidence-based management actions.

This course provides detailed assessment protocols, scoring scales, interpretation guidance, and management responses for each component — grounded in peer-reviewed literature and authoritative industry management guides.

  • Evidence-based content from Merck Vet Manual, NFACC, CFIA, Ross & Cobb breed manuals
  • APA-cited references throughout
  • Image placeholders for each T-FLAWS component
  • Curated peer-reviewed journal list appended
  • Designed for broiler, layer, and breeder operations
